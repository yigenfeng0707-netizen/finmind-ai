## Q&A Preparation

### Q: How is this different from ChatGPT?
**A:** ChatGPT is a general-purpose LLM. FinMind is a **specialized multi-agent system**. We have 4 domain-specific agents with a coordinator. ChatGPT gives you a black-box answer. We show you **exactly how** the decision was made, agent by agent. And we're **risk-first** — ChatGPT won't tell you when *not* to invest.

### Q: How accurate is it?
**A:** We're not a crystal ball — and we don't pretend to be. We provide **70% average confidence** on a 0-100% scale, plus **target prices and stop-losses** so users know the downside. We are transparent about uncertainty.

### Q: What's your data source?
**A:** Yahoo Finance for real-time market data. NewsAPI for global financial news. Both have **mock fallback** layers for reliability.

### Q: Can you make money with this?
**A:** Yes. **$50B+ AI-in-finance market**. Our freemium model is proven: free tier drives adoption, Pro at $49/month serves active traders, Enterprise at $299/month serves funds. We're targeting the **99% of investors** locked out of Bloomberg's $24K tier.

### Q: Why financial services?
**A:** Three reasons. One: **massive market** ($50B+). Two: **clear ROI** for users (better investment decisions). Three: **clear differentiator** — multi-agent transparency is unique in this space.

### Q: How do you handle market volatility?
**A:** That's exactly what our **Risk Agent** does. It quantifies volatility in real time, recommends position sizing, and provides stop-loss levels. We **don't ignore risk** — we lead with it.

### Q: Is this open source? Why?
**A:** Yes, **MIT license**. Why? Because **transparency is our differentiator**. We want the community to audit our code, contribute agents, and trust the system. Closed-source financial AI is a black box. We're a glass box.

### Q: Can I integrate this into my app?
**A:** Yes! The **Enterprise tier** includes full API access, webhooks, and white-label options. We designed FinMind to be **extensible** — new agents plug in easily.

### Q: How long did this take to build?
**A:** **30 days**. From idea to production deployment. The full timeline is on our GitHub. This is what focused execution looks like.

### Q: What's next?
**A:** **3 priorities**: (1) Real-time data, (2) Multi-language UI, (3) Mobile app. Then crypto, then institutional platform. We're building the **Bloomberg of the 21st century** — open, transparent, and free.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ Reading slides — judges can read
2. ❌ Going over 5 minutes — practice with a timer
3. ❌ Demo failing — always have a backup
4. ❌ Technical jargon dump — speak simply
5. ❌ Forgetting to mention "open source" — judges love this
6. ❌ Weak close — practice the "Try it. Star it. Ship it." line
7. ❌ No eye contact — look at judges, not screen
8. ❌ Filler words ("um", "uh") — pause instead

---

- [ ] Stand tall, shoulders back
